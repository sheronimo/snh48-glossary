## d7, d7b, d7boys, dsb, d7 youth league (D7 少年团)

**Definition**: D7BOYS, a boy group under Star48

**Notes**: also known as Double Seven Boys, D 七少年团

## dahao, tuba (大号)

**Definition**: main account, primary social media account

**Notes**: 大号 (dà hào) = tuba

## daiwang, bag king, pocket king (袋王)

**Definition**: Pocket48 admin team

**Notes**: 袋王 (dài wáng) = pocket king

## dalao, dl, boss, big guy (大佬), boss fan, big fan (大佬粉)

**Definition**: wealthy fans who spend large amounts of money on votes and gifts for idols

**Notes**: 大佬 (dàlǎo) = big guy, big boss, gangster
