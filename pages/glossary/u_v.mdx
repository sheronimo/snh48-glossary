## u1s1, y1s1

**Definition**: to tell you the truth, speaking honestly

**Notes**: 有一说一 (yǒu yī shuō yī) = to speak the truth

## under girls, ug, top32, goofy group (高飞组)

**Definition**: members who ranked in elections from #17 to #32

**Notes**: 高飞组 (gāo fēi zǔ) = Fly High Group

## underground idol (地下偶像), earth puppet, diou (地偶), indie idol, chika idol, live idol

**Definition**: idols that do not appear in mass media and are not signed with a company

**Notes**: from the Japanese 地下アイドル (chika aidoru) = underground idol

## unspeakable (不可说), unspeakable CP (不可说 CP), bks

**Definition**: famous SNH48 Group CPs that ended on infamously bad terms

**Notes**: 不可说 (bù kě shuō) = unspeakable

## unspeakable 1.0, bks 1.0 (不可说 1.0)

**Definition**: Kahuang (卡黄), the CP of Li Yitong and Huang Tingting

**Notes**: 不可说 1.0 (bù kě shuō 1.0) = unspeakable #1

## unspeakable 2.0, bks 2.0 (不可说 2.0)

**Definition**: Heimiao (黑喵), the CP of Yuan Yiqi and Shen Mengyao

**Notes**: 不可说 2.0 (bù kě shuō 2.0) = unspeakable #2
