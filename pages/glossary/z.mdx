## zanxiu, hiatus (暂休)

**Definition**: SNH48 Group members that have effectively graduated from their group and are not currently active as idols, but are still under contract with Siba

**Notes**: 暂休 (zàn xiū) = on hiatus, on break

## zbzy

**Definition**: capitalism

**Notes**: 资本主义 (zīběn zhǔyì) = capitalism

## zc

**Definition**: self-harm

**Notes**: 自残 (zì cán) = self-harm

## zdl

**Definition**: "understood", "I got it"

**Notes**: 知道了 (zhīdàole) = understood

## zhainan, nerd (宅男)

**Definition**: male otaku

**Notes**: 宅男 (zháinán) = male otaku
